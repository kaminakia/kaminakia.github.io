Welcome to Kaminakia, a sanctuary nestled amidst the serene beauty of Fiskardo.

At the northern tip of Kefalonia, where the Ionian Sea softly embraces the shore, Kaminakia Apartments invites you to slow down.

Set just above a small cove, our adults-only apartments offer a haven of calm in Fiscardo.
Let the ancient olive and fragrant lemon trees shade your thoughts, linger beside the serene pool, take in the endless sea view from your balcony, or simply surrender to the tender hush that flows through the surroundings.

A serene hideaway where simplicity meets comfort, Kaminakia is designed exclusively for those seeking calm and connection in Kefalonia.
And more than just a place to stay, Kaminakia is woven as a quiet invitation: to rest deeply, to read contemplatively, to breathe freely,

...to feel time stretch like a sunbeam through your window.

Our philosophy: warmth, functionality, and quietude.

Every corner of Kaminakia invites quiet contemplation and restful unfolding - an environment where both soul and body can breath with ease.

We believe in sophisticated simplicity – subtle touches that ensure our haven feels inviting and lived-in, never sterile or cold, always in harmony with the surrounding landscape.

With apartments thoughtfully designed for comfort, and a refreshing pool nestled among an evergreen cypress forest, Kaminakia emerges like an oasis - where each passing moment becomes a gentle step toward inner serenity.

Location - Blessed by Nature

Just a peaceful stroll away from the picturesque port of Fiscardo and the shimmering waters of its most beloved beaches. And yet here, the world seems to pause, as if exhaling, and time loosens its grip...

From your veranda, the sea stretches wide, a canvas of shifting blues, where the hush of waves speaks in soft rhythms, lulling you into stillness.

Below, orange and lemon trees sway in the breeze, their blossoms sweetening the air with quiet joy. The scent, the sound, the view — each a gentle reminder: you’ve arrived in a place where time ripens slowly.

Choose Your Perfect Sanctuary

At our accommodation, we offer three distinct lodging options, each designed to provide a unique and comfortable experience.

While these descriptions offer guidance, please choose the option that best suits your needs and preferences.

1. Studio (21m²): The Explorer’s Nest

Compact and economical, our studios are nestled on the upper row.

They're perfect for those who love to explore the island by day and return to a peaceful sanctuary where they can whip up a simple meal and unwind after a full day.

  • Small kitchenette
  • WC
  • Bedroom with a queen size bed
  • Your private, panoramic sea-view veranda
  • Air conditioning (A/C)

2. Apartment 1-4 (42m²): The 4R's - Read, Relish, Rest, Repeat

More spacious and located on the front row, these Apartments are ideal for guests who cherish time at their 'home away from home.'

Here, you'll discover ample room to truly stretch out, find your quiet corner, and settle in.

Whether you're savoring a leisurely homemade dinner, diving into a good book, or simply unwinding with a quiet drink as the day gently fades into evening, this space is designed for those peaceful mornings and serene evenings where the greatest luxury is the quiet comfort of your own space.

  • A larger kitchen
  • WC
  • Bedroom with a queen size bed
  • A living room with comfortable sofa beds and a ceiling fan
  • A generous sea-view veranda
  • Air conditioning (A/C)

3. Apartment 6-7 (42m²): A More Exclusive Offering

This option offers an added layer of seclusion and tranquility.

As upper-floor, leftmost units, they provide enhanced privacy along with truly stunning views – a perfect spot for quiet contemplation.

  • All features of Apartment 1-4
  • Elevated sea view
